  • I Have No Mouth And I Must Scream — Lost_Elf

    Jack groans in discomfort. Or tries to. The air barely passes through his throat in the right way, and no sound is let out of his mouth. He reaches up to his throat, that also feels wrong – and fortunately, he is not tied to the bed, because that would be a real cliché – and massages it. Wrong.
    He tries to make another sound, speak, cough, whistle. And that is when he finally notices.
    Jack’s hands shoot up to his face, searching, touching, but it just, it just isn’t there! He has no mouth. And no voice cords, probably, but he has no mouth, and how is he going to speak, eat, live, oh God—
    Panic is almost new to Handsome Jack. He claws at his face, forcibly tries to move his jaw, but it doesn’t budge.
